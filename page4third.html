choose a path

You and the fierce crab begin on your journey. You stop seeing two different directions you can take. One leads to the beach's edge where you will encounter humans and crabs alike. The other is a direct path to the cluster of rocks where you will be able to relax in peace and watch the waves crash into the bottom of the rock. Which path will you take?
